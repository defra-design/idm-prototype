Use a single Defra account to access multiple Defra services.

Defra account uses a Government Gateway account as your sign in. Also when you have your Defra account you will need to enrol it to the services you want to use.

Steps to prepare your Defra account

  1. Step 1 Sign into a Government Gateway account

    Sign up for, or use a Government Gateway account already associated with your organisation.

  2. Step 2 Create your Defra account

    Add more detailed information about your organisation.

  3. Step 3 Enrol the new Defra account to your service

    Link your Defra account to the services you want to use it with.
