Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer that collect information about how you browse the service.

They are used to:

  • measure how you use the service so it can be updated and improved based on user needs
  • remember notifications you’ve seen so that you’re not shown them again

GOV.UK cookies are not used to personally identify you.

Cookies that are strictly necessary

These cookies are essential to make this service run and to help us stay in line with data protection laws. You cannot turn these cookies off.
