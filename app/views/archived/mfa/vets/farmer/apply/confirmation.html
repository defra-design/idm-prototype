What you should do next

Contact the vet to book the review. You’ll need to give the vet your reference number.

The vet will contact you to organise testing of your livestock. They will test for:

  • bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) in dairy and beef cattle
  • how effective worming treatments are in sheep
  • porcine reproductive and respiratory syndrome virus (PRRS) in pigs

The vet will discuss the test results and treatment options with you when they visit.

Farm visit

The vet will visit your farm to carry out the review. Discuss with the vet what you want to focus on.

Confirm the record and get paid

After the vet has recorded information about the review, you’ll receive an email asking you to confirm the record is correct. Sign in to confirm the record and claim funding for an annual health and welfare review of your livestock.

You’ll get your payment within 28 days.

Get help with your application

If you have a question about your application contact the Rural Payments Agency. Have your reference with you when you contact us.

  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
