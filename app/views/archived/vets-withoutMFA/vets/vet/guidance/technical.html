Details

Any vet or vet-led team conducting an annual health and welfare review of livestock needs to follow the technical guidance for testing and use an approved laboratory for sample analysis.

The technical guidance will help you understand the species-specific:

  • data you need to collect
  • test you need to carry out
  • sample analysis requirements

Read the carry out an annual health and welfare review of livestock guidance before you read this technical guidance.
