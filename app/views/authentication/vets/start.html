Before you start

You’ll need your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in.

You must be eligible to apply for an annual health and welfare review. Read the guidance on getting a funded annual health and welfare review of your livestock before you use this service.

Get help with your application

If you cannot apply online or have a question about your application, contact the Rural Payments Agency by email or phone.

Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
