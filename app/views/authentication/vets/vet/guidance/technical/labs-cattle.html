Guidance

Approved laboratories to test for bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) in cattle

Published 12 July 2022

Vets must send samples to a laboratory on this list that has been approved by the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra).

More laboratories may be added at a later date.
