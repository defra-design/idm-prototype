Before you start

You'll need the:

  • reference number Defra gave the farmer when they applied
  • vet's Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons (RCVS) registration number
  • name of the vet who did the review
  • name of the vet practice
  • email address of the vet practice
  • date of the review
  • postcode where review took place
  • disease and endemic conditions test results
  • number of animals reviewed
