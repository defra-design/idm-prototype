Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website. We use 2 types of cookies on this site:

  • cookies that are strictly necessary
  • cookies that measure service usage

Cookies that are strictly necessary

These cookies are essential to make this service run and to help us stay in line with data protection laws. You cannot turn these cookies off.

Introductory cookie message

You may see a pop-up welcome message when you first visit. A cookie is stored so that your computer knows you’ve seen it and knows not to show it again.

Name Purpose Expires

Google reCAPTCHA cookies

We use Google reCAPTCHA on this site to make sure that information (for example, in forms) is entered by a human and not by automated software. Some of the data collected by reCAPTCHA cookies is forwarded to Google. See the Google Privacy Policy (opens in new tab) and the Google Terms of Service (opens in new tab).

Cookies that measure your use of the service

These cookies track the pages you visit here, how long you spend on each page and the options you choose. You can turn these cookies on or off.

We use software (Google Universal Analytics and Microsoft Application Insights) to collect information about how you use the service.

This information is ’anonymised’, we don’t know who it’s about.

We do not collect or store your personal information (for example, your name or address) and we do not allow Google or Microsoft to use our data or share it with anyone else.

We collect this information to make sure the service is meeting your needs and to help us improve it.
